About the Author

Jerome "Jerry" Seinfeld is an American stand-up comedian, actor, writer, and television/film producer, best known for playing a semi-fictional version of himself in the sitcom Seinfeld, which he co-created and co-wrote with Larry David, and, in the show's final two seasons, co-executive-produced.
